Env Graph

Infer, Don’t Hand-Wire

Durable Debt

Replace TODO Fragments

Persist operator debt as structured records instead of markdown fragments or CC-switch-local state. The menu should expose backlog, blockers, and required user input.

  • `todo_item` with scope, severity, and source
  • `todo_event` for review and closure history
  • Portable local-first store, replication later
